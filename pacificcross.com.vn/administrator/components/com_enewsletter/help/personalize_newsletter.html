Personalize Newsletter

Introduction

  • Type in the title, introduction and trailer for your e-newsletter.
  • The title will appear at the top of your e-newsletter and as the subject line of the email.
  • The introduction will appear below the title.
  • The trailer will appear at the bottom of the e-newsletter below the stories you choose.
  • When you are done editing click on SAVE & SELECT STORIES button to save and go to the next step.

Select Article

Introduction

  • Select the articles for your newsletter by clicking the checkbox.
  • You can select up to four articles for your newsletter.
  • Once you have finished selecting the articles go to next step to select groups/subscribers.
